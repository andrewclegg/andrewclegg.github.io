About

I’m a data scientist and machine learning engineer, and this is my blog.

I’m based in London, and I work at Yelp. Most of what I do these days is related to recommendations, search quality, personalization, and other ways to improve user experience through data mining. I’ve previously worked at Etsy, Pearson, Last.fm, Smesh, the University of London’s Institute of Structural and Molecular Biology, and AstraZeneca, among others. My academic background is in natural language processing, text mining and bioinformatics. Have a look at my talks and workshops page for more on my projects inside and outside of working hours.
